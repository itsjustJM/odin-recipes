Cinnamon Roll

Ingredients

For Rolls

  • 1 cup of warm milk
  • 2 eggs (Room temp)
  • 1/3 cup of margarine (Melted)
  • 4 1/2 cups of bread flour
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 cup of white sugar
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons of bread yeast

For Sugar and Cinnamon mixture

  • 1 cup of brown sugar (Packed)
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons of ground cinnamon
  • 1/3 cup of softened butter

For Frosting

  • 1 (3 ounce) package of softened cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup of softened butter
  • 1 1/2 cups of confectioners sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/8 teaspoon of salt

Directions

  1. Place ingredients in the pan of the bread machine in the order recommended by the by the manufacturer. Select Dough Cycle; Press start.
  2. After the dough has doubled in size, turn it onto a lightly floured surface, cover and let rest for 10 minutes.
  3. In a small bowl mix together the brown sugar and cinnamon.
  4. Roll the dough into a 16 inch by 21 inch rectangle. Spread the 1/3 cup of butter onto the dough and sprinkle evenly with the brown sugar and cinnamon mixture.
  5. Roll up the dough and cut into 12 rolls.
  6. Place rolls in a lightly greased 9 inch by 13 inch baking pan. Cover and let rise until nearly doubled.
  7. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F
  8. Bake rolls in oven until golden brown.
  9. While the rolls bake, beat together the cream cheese, 1/4 cup of butter, confectioners sugar, vanilla extract and salt. Spread the frosting on warm rolls before serving.
