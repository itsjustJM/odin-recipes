Peanut Butter Cookie

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butted (room temp)
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 3/4 cup of brown sugar
  • 2 large legs (room temp)
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 1 cup of creamy peanut butter
  • 3 cups plus 2 tablespoons of all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
    • 1/3 cup of sugar (for rolling, optional)

Directions

  1. Cream the softened butter and both sugars together until smooth
  2. Add the egg and mix until combined
  3. Add the vanilla and peanut butter and mix until combined
  4. In a separte bowl, toss the flour, baking soda, baking powder and salt together.
  5. Add to the wet Ingredients and mix until combined. Dough will be thick yet sticky.
  6. Cover tightly and chill for 3 hours
  7. Preheat over to 350 degrees F
  8. Line two large baking sheets with parchment paper or silicone baking mats
  9. Roll balls of dough (1.5 tablespoons per cookie) into sugar.
  10. Place balls on baking sheet 2 inches apart. Pressing a fork into the top, giving the cookie that classic Peanut Butter Cookie look.
  11. bake for 11 minutes or until very lightly browned on the sides.
  12. Remove from oven and let cool for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
