Rocky Road Fudge

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup unsalted butter, cut into chunks
  • 1 cup evaporated milk
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 12 ounces semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup slivered almonds (roasted)
  • 1 cup mini marshmellows

Directions

  1. Combine butter, evaporated milk, sugar and salt in a medium saucepan. Bring to a hard boil for 5-7 minutes, stirring constantly.
  2. Remove from heat
  3. Stire in the chocolate chips until melted smooth.
  4. Fold in the almonds and the marshmallows.
  5. Pour into an 8-inch by 8-inch square baking pan.
  6. Chill until firm.
  7. Cut into 12 squares.
